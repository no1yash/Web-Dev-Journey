Sprouts and Vegetable Salad

This salad is made of fresh sprouts and vegetables, seasoned with chaat masala.

Ingredients

  • Soaked Sprouts
  • Vegetables
  • Chat Masala
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Soak the sprouts for at least two days.
  2. Wash the vegetables before eating.
  3. Add masala if needed.
  4. Do not use frozen ingredients.
